## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the application home page
2. Click "Register" button
3. Fill in your details:
   - **Full Name**: Your complete name
   - **Email**: Your personal email
   - **LASU Email**: Your LASU student/staff email
   - **Phone**: Your contact number
   - **Password**: Create a strong password
   - **Role**: Select your role (Customer, Vendor, or Admin)
4. Click "Sign Up"
5. You'll be logged in automatically

### Logging In

1. Click "Login" on the home page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### Resetting Your Password

1. On the login page, click "Forgot Password?"
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. You can now login with your new password

## Customer Guide

### Finding Products

#### Browse by Category
1. Go to the "Shop" page
2. Select a category from the left sidebar
3. Products in that category will display

#### Search for Products
1. Use the search bar at the top of any page
2. Type the product name or keywords
3. Press Enter or click search
4. Relevant products will appear

#### Filter and Sort
- **Sort by**: Price Low to High, Price High to Low, Newest, Most Popular, Highest Rated
- **Price Range**: Use the price filter to set min and max price
- **Category**: Filter by specific category
- **Rating**: Filter by minimum rating

### Viewing Product Details

1. Click on any product card
2. You'll see:
   - Product images (gallery with zoom)
   - Product name and price
   - Vendor information
   - Rating and number of reviews
   - Description and specifications
   - Available quantity
   - Customer reviews and ratings
   - Add to Cart button

### Adding Products to Cart

1. On the product detail page, select quantity
2. Click "Add to Cart"
3. A notification will confirm the item was added
4. The cart badge in the header shows cart count

### Managing Your Cart

1. Click the shopping cart icon in the header
2. View your cart items:
   - Product name and price
   - Quantity (use +/- to adjust)
   - Remove button
3. Scroll down to see:
   - Subtotal
   - Shipping fee
   - **Total Price**
4. Click "Proceed to Checkout" to continue

### Checkout Process

1. **Shipping Address**:
   - Enter your delivery address
   - Full name, street, city, state, postal code
   - Phone number for delivery

2. **Payment Method**:
   - Select Paystack (online payment)
   - Or Cash on Delivery (if available)

3. **Payment**:
   - For Paystack: You'll be redirected to Paystack
   - Enter your card details
   - Complete the payment
   - Return to confirmation page

4. **Order Confirmation**:
   - Your order number will display
   - You'll receive an email confirmation
   - Order will appear in "My Orders"

### Managing Orders

**View All Orders:**
1. Click your profile icon → "My Orders"
2. See all your orders with status:
   - **Pending**: Vendor hasn't confirmed
   - **Processing**: Vendor is preparing
   - **Shipped**: Item is in transit
   - **Delivered**: Order received
   - **Cancelled**: Order was cancelled

**Track an Order:**
1. Click on any order from the list
2. See order details including:
   - Order items
   - Shipping address
   - Current status
   - Tracking information (if available)

**Cancel an Order:**
1. On the order detail page
2. If status is "Pending", click "Cancel Order"
3. Provide a reason for cancellation
4. Payment will be refunded

### Writing Reviews

1. After receiving an order, go to "My Orders"
2. Click on the delivered order
3. Click "Write Review" under each product
4. Provide:
   - **Star Rating** (1-5 stars)
   - **Review Title** (short summary)
   - **Review Content** (detailed feedback)
   - **Images** (optional - attach photos)
5. Click "Submit Review"

### Messaging Vendors

1. On a product detail page, click "Contact Vendor"
2. Or go to "Messages" in your profile menu
3. Type your message
4. Include product ID or order ID for context
5. Click "Send"
6. Vendor will receive your message
7. Check "Messages" to see vendor responses

### Viewing Account Profile

1. Click your profile icon → "Profile"
2. View/Edit:
   - Full name
   - Email addresses
   - Phone number
   - Shipping address
   - Profile picture (optional)
3. Click "Edit" to change information
4. Click "Save" to confirm changes

### Notifications

1. Bell icon in header shows notification count
2. Types of notifications:
   - Order status updates
   - Review responses from vendors
   - New messages
   - Payment confirmations
   - Admin announcements
3. Click notification to take action
4. Mark as read or delete notifications

## Vendor Guide

### Setting Up Your Vendor Account

1. Register as a Vendor
2. Complete your vendor profile:
   - **Shop Name**: Your business name
   - **Shop Description**: What you sell
   - **Contact Phone**: Business contact
   - **Shop Address**: Business location
   - **Shop Logo** (optional)
3. Submit for admin approval
4. Wait for admin verification email

### Creating Products

1. Go to "Vendor Dashboard" → "Products"
2. Click "Add New Product"
3. Fill in product details:
   - **Product Name**
   - **Description**: Detailed product info
   - **Category**: Select from list
   - **Price**: Base price in Naira
   - **Quantity**: Available stock
   - **SKU**: Product code (optional)
   - **Discount %**: Set percentage discount
   - **Images**: Upload up to 5 photos
   - **Tags**: Add searchable tags
4. Click "Save Product"
5. Admin will review and approve
6. Once approved, product goes live

### Managing Inventory

1. Go to "Vendor Dashboard" → "Inventory"
2. See list of all your products with:
   - Current quantity
   - Sales this month
   - Rating
   - Status (Active/Inactive)
3. Click product to edit:
   - Update quantity
   - Change price
   - Adjust discount
   - Update description
   - Modify images
4. Click "Save Changes"
5. **Low Stock Alert**: System alerts when items drop below 5

### Processing Orders

1. Go to "Vendor Dashboard" → "Orders"
2. See all orders containing your products
3. For each order:
   - **Status**: Current stage
   - **Customer**: Who ordered
   - **Items**: Products ordered
   - **Total**: Order amount
4. Click order to view details
5. Update order status:
   - Mark as "Processing" when preparing
   - Mark as "Shipped" when sending
   - Add tracking number if available
6. Customer receives notification for each status change

### Viewing Performance

1. Go to "Vendor Dashboard" → "Analytics"
2. See key metrics:
   - **Total Sales**: Revenue this month
   - **Total Orders**: Number of orders
   - **Average Rating**: Your shop rating
   - **Products Sold**: Total units sold
3. Sales chart showing trend over time
4. Top selling products list

### Managing Reviews

1. Go to "Vendor Dashboard" → "Reviews"
2. See all reviews on your products
3. For each review:
   - Read customer feedback
   - See rating given
   - See date posted
4. Respond to reviews (optional)
5. Report inappropriate reviews to admin

### Customer Messaging

1. Go to "Messages" in your dashboard
2. See list of conversations with customers
3. Click conversation to view messages
4. Send responses to customer inquiries
5. Include product/order references for clarity
6. **Response Time**: Try to respond within 24 hours

### Updating Vendor Profile

1. Go to "Vendor Dashboard" → "Settings" → "Profile"
2. Edit:
   - Shop name and description
   - Contact information
   - Business hours
   - Shop policies
   - Shop logo
3. Save changes

### Withdrawal and Payments

1. Go to "Vendor Dashboard" → "Payments"
2. See your earnings:
   - **Available Balance**: Ready to withdraw
   - **Pending Balance**: From recent orders
3. Request withdrawal:
   - Set amount
   - Provide bank details
   - Submit request
4. Admin processes payment within 5-7 business days

## Admin Guide

### Admin Dashboard

1. Login as admin
2. Go to "Admin Dashboard"
3. Main view shows:
   - Total users, vendors, customers
   - Total orders and revenue
   - Recent activities
   - Key metrics

### Managing Users

1. Go to "Dashboard" → "Users"
2. View all registered users
3. Filter by role (Admin, Vendor, Customer)
4. For each user, see:
   - Name and email
   - Registration date
   - Total purchases/sales
   - Account status

**User Actions:**
- Click user to view profile
- Suspend account (if needed)
- View user's order history
- View user's product listings
- Send notifications to user

### Approving Vendors

1. Go to "Dashboard" → "Vendor Requests"
2. See pending vendor applications
3. Review each application:
   - Business name
   - Description
   - Contact information
   - Qualifications
4. **Approve**: Click "Approve" → vendor can start selling
5. **Reject**: Click "Reject" → provide reason
6. Vendor receives notification

### Managing Products

1. Go to "Dashboard" → "Products"
2. See all products in system
3. Filter by:
   - Status (Approved, Pending, Rejected)
   - Vendor
   - Category
4. For each product:
   - View details
   - Check for policy violations
   - **Approve**: Click "Approve" → goes live
   - **Reject**: Click "Reject" → provide reason to vendor
   - **Remove**: Delete inappropriate products

### Monitoring Orders

1. Go to "Dashboard" → "Orders"
2. See all orders in system
3. Track status distribution
4. Investigate issues:
   - Undelivered orders
   - Customer disputes
   - Refund requests
5. Contact vendor/customer if needed

### Managing Categories

1. Go to "Dashboard" → "Categories"
2. View all product categories
3. Add new category:
   - Category name
   - Description
   - Category image
4. Edit existing:
   - Update name/description
   - Change category image
5. Remove category (if no products using it)

### Viewing Reports

1. Go to "Dashboard" → "Reports"
2. Available reports:
   - **Sales Report**: Revenue trends, top products, top vendors
   - **User Report**: Registration trends, active users
   - **Order Report**: Order volume, delivery times
   - **Product Report**: Most viewed, most reviewed
3. Export reports to CSV for analysis

### System Settings

1. Go to "Dashboard" → "Settings"
2. Configure:
   - **Commission Rate**: % platform takes from vendor sales
   - **Shipping Fees**: Base shipping cost
   - **Policies**: Terms of service, refund policy
   - **Maintenance Mode**: Take site down for updates
   - **Email Templates**: Customize notification emails

### Handling Disputes

1. Go to "Dashboard" → "Disputes"
2. See reported issues:
   - Order disputes
   - Product quality issues
   - Payment problems
3. Review evidence from both parties
4. Make decision:
   - Refund customer
   - Compensate vendor
   - Take no action
5. Send resolution to both parties

### User Support

1. Go to "Dashboard" → "Support"
2. See messages from users
3. Reply to support queries
4. Track resolution status
5. Create FAQ entries for common issues

### System Logs

1. Go to "Dashboard" → "Logs"
2. View system activity:
   - Login attempts
   - Product uploads
   - Payment transactions
   - Errors and warnings
3. Filter by date, user, or event type
4. Use for troubleshooting and auditing

## Best Practices

### For Customers
- Read product reviews before purchasing
- Check vendor ratings
- Provide detailed delivery address
- Keep order numbers for reference
- Write honest reviews to help others

### For Vendors
- Keep products well-stocked
- Respond quickly to customer messages
- Upload clear product images
- Keep product information accurate
- Maintain high customer service standards

### For Admins
- Review products promptly
- Monitor for policy violations
- Respond to support tickets quickly
- Keep system updated
- Back up data regularly

## Troubleshooting

### Payment Issues
- Ensure card is valid
- Check bank for transaction limits
- Try different payment method
- Contact support if problem persists

### Product Not Appearing
- Check if admin has approved
- Verify product quantity > 0
- Check product category
- Wait up to 24 hours for approval

### Order Not Delivered
- Check tracking information
- Contact vendor for update
- Escalate to admin if needed
- Request refund if necessary

### Account Access Issues
- Use password reset if forgot password
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try different browser
- Contact support for help
